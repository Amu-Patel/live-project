About

Quisine is a platform connecting Home Chefs with the market. We are on a mission for creating
empowerment opportunities for homemakers and unemployed who can engage themselves with Quisine. One can order tiffin,
meals, fast food, snacks and beverages which will pe prepared by Home based chefs and delivered by Quisine. Subscribers
of tiffin services of Quisine will have the option to choose everyday meal in their tiffin as per their choice. The
preparations will be hygienic and healthy as compared to those prepared at restaurants and food outlets. Also, the
kitchens will be monitored by Quisine such that kitchens are maintained clean and hygienic.
